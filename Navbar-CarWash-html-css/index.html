The Suli Transi Story

Born in the heart of our community, built on trust, and driven by excellence

Our Beginning

Founded by local entrepreneur Suli Mthembu in 2019, Suli Transi started as a simple dream: to bring professional car care services to our township community while creating local jobs and fostering connections between neighbors.

Community First

What makes us special isn't just our spotless results – it's our genuine care for every customer. We know your name, remember your car's preferences, and treat every vehicle like it belongs to family. That's the township way.

Excellence & Growth

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into the area's most trusted car care destination. We've invested in the latest eco-friendly equipment and trained our team to deliver premium results that rival any high-end facility.

Our Mission

"To provide exceptional car care services that reflect the warmth and pride of our community, while creating opportunities for local growth and fostering lasting relationships with our customers."
